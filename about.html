NGO-Hub

Who We Are

The NGO-hub Foundation is the platform for the NGOs to reduce their efforts in contacting volunteers and to expand their work areas and their service.
It also helps the NGOs to post their work in this website. It also helps new volunteers to join new NGOs and serve more to the people. This website helps the NGOs to expand more and gives them a platform to serve more.
